Chanting the dread praises of the Lord of Murder, you are filled with the spirit of His favoured children and your foes look upon one of the many faces of death.

Ingredients: Five strands of Manticore fur.

Praising the name of the Lord of Murder He grants you his blessing by infusing you with the vicious and bloodthirsty spirit of the Manticore. You gain the Terror (Willpower Bonus) creature trait from your monstrous visage and the manifest blessing of the Lord of Murder grants your allies Fearless (All) talent within Willpower Bonus yards of you.
