The sorceress conjures a whirling vortex of devastating magical energy that drags her victims into one of the infernal regions.

Black Horror is a magical missile targeting everyone in the area of effect. Targets within the area of effect must succeed in an Opposed Athletics/Language (Magick) test or suffer +15 damage, ignoring Armour Points and Toughness Bonus. Targets within the area of effect must make an immediate Cool test against Terror (Willpower Bonus rating).
