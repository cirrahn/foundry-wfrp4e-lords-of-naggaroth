Heavy bladed sabres favoured by the corsairs aboard Naggaroths Black Arks and raiding fleets. Elegantly curved in a way that strongly favours the cut over the thrust and usually forged with complex handguards that ranges from simple knuckle-bows to basket hilts of Druchii design. They are often lavished upon as symbols of status and no Corsair captain or Black Ark fleetmaster would be seen without one. Often paired with matching parrying daggers.
