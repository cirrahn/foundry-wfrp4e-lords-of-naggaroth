An archaic weapon for ritual combat in dueling between the nobility, paired with the Ghlaith. The name ‘Lakelui’ means soultaker in the Druchii tongue and is used to dispatch the foe once they have been rendered defenseless by the Ghlaith. Exceptionally rare in the Old World, it does find some favour with the Cult of Khaine. As the heavy blade, fitted on a long haft much like a short glaive, makes it well suited for ritual sacrifices or dramatic killing blows in murders.
