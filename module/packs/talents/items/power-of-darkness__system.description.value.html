The raw energy of magic can be immensly powerful, but it is highly unstable and can be as dangerous to the wielder as it is to the foe.

Channeling the volatile flows of dhar requires an absolute will to do without dangerous backlash, however in the quest for ever more power Druchii sorceresses have learned to tax their own flesh or even bargain part of their souls to cast spells of even greater potency. At the cost of two wounds before casting a spell from the Lore of Dark Magic, the Sorceress may add their Willpower Bonus number of SL’s to their Casting test. At the cost of one corruption point the Sorceress may add double their Willpower Bonus number of SL’s to their Casting test.
